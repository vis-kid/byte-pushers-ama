AMA Meetups?

Together with Edenspiekermann and Google we will host regular “Ask Me Anything” events at Google SG. The public is invited to join us free of charge and will get access to the brainpower of high-profile guests from the design, programming and startup circles.

We not only invite professionals from these industries to join our AMA sessions but we welcome kids and students as our guests as well. We are convinced that it is highly important to inspire the next generation of makers through the exposure to interesting, forward-thinking individuals and their insights.
